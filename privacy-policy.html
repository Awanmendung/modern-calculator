Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 14, 2026
Last Updated: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Modern Calculator. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator application ("the App").

TL;DR - Quick Summary:

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Your privacy is 100% protected because we simply don't gather any data about you.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

Modern Calculator is designed with privacy as a core principle. The App:

  • Does not require registration, login, or account creation
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not access your device contacts, photos, or files
  • Does not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not collect IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Does not use analytics or crash reporting services

3. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information about you, we cannot use, share, or sell your data. All calculations are processed locally on your device.

4. Data Storage

The only data stored by Modern Calculator is:

  • Calculation History: Stored locally in your device's browser storage (localStorage)
  • App Cache: For offline functionality using Service Workers

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or app data.

5. Third-Party Services

Modern Calculator does not integrate with any third-party services for:

  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Payment processors (the app is free)

6. Advertising

Modern Calculator is completely ad-free. We do not display advertisements, and we do not work with advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

Modern Calculator is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any information, there is no risk to children's privacy. Parents can feel confident letting their children use this app.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

8. Data Security

While we don't collect personal data, we still take security seriously:

  • All connections to our web app use HTTPS encryption
  • Your calculation data stays on your device
  • We regularly update the app to fix any security vulnerabilities

9. Your Rights

Because we don't collect any personal data, typical data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However:

  • You can clear your local calculation history anytime from within the app
  • You can uninstall the app at any time
  • You can clear app data through your device settings

10. International Users

Modern Calculator can be used anywhere in the world. Since we don't collect data, there are no concerns about international data transfers or compliance with regional regulations like GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through the app if the changes are material
  • Post the new policy on our website

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
